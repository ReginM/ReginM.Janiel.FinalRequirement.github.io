GROWTH MINDSET

"Hi, I am Brynna Janiel L. Potenciano, I am already in my last year of college life and currently, I am undertaking an “On Job Training” or “OJT”. Today I will be discussing my point of view on how I see a “Growth Mindset” as an individual, But first? What is Growth Mindset? "

" Firstly, growth mindsets believe that self-development abilities and intelligence can be developed through dedication and hard work. On the other hand, fixed thinking is the belief that one's abilities and intelligence are confined to stone and cannot be changed. Growth mindsets tend to accept challenges, persevere through obstacles, and learn from criticism, while fixed mindsets tend to avoid challenges, quickly give up and consider criticism to be personal attacks. "

" In her book Mindset: The New Psychology of Success, psychologist Carol Dweck popularized the concept of the growth mindset. Dweck's study showed that people with growth thinking tend to achieve more in academia, sports, and others areas than people with fixed thinking. People with growth mindsets see challenges as opportunities for learning and growth, not threats to intelligence or abilities. I believe that a growth mindset is key to personal and professional development. When we have a growth mindset, we are more likely to take risks, try new things, and achieve greater success and satisfaction in life. We also tend to persevere through difficulties rather than give up when the first signs of difficulties arise. "

" One aspect of a growth mindset that I find especially valuable is the emphasis on learning from failure. Failure, when viewed through the lens of a growth mindset, is viewed as an opportunity to learn and grow rather than a reflection of our abilities or intelligence. This is extremely empowering because it allows us to see setbacks as stepping stones to success rather than obstacles. The emphasis on effort rather than talent is another aspect of a growth mindset that I value. When we have a growth mindset, we understand that hard work and dedication are frequently more important than natural ability. This can be a great equalizer because it means that anyone who is persistent and eager to learn can succeed. "

" Now on my part, a growth mindset can be extremely beneficial as an OJT trainee in the I.T. business field. The field of information technology is known for its difficulties and obstacles, and having a growth mindset can help you see these difficulties as opportunities to learn and grow. When you have a growth mindset, you can see feedback and criticism as opportunities to improve rather than as a criticism of your abilities. Persistence is also important in the IT industry, and having a growth mindset can help you persevere and overcome obstacles. A growth mindset can also assist you in approaching new technologies with curiosity and eagerness to learn, rather than fear. Finally, in the information technology industry, collaboration is extremely important, and a growth mindset encourages open communication and a willingness to learn from others. Overall, developing the skills and attitudes required to succeed in the information technology industry can be aided by having a growth mindset "

" Is it that important that I should a growth mindset? Yes, an IT OJT trainee requires a growth mindset. The field of information technology is always evolving, and new technologies are constantly being introduced. As an OJT student in IT, you will face a variety of challenges and opportunities to learn new skills. If you have a growth mindset, you can approach these challenges and opportunities with a positive attitude and willingness to learn. In addition, as an IT OJT trainee, you will almost certainly receive performance feedback from your supervisor or coworkers. A growth mindset can help you see this feedback as an opportunity to learn and grow, rather than a criticism of your abilities. Furthermore, accepting and incorporating feedback into your work can help you learn new skills and become a more effective IT professional. Furthermore, in the IT industry, working in teams is common, and having a growth mindset can help you collaborate effectively with your coworkers. By valuing the perspectives and ideas of others, you can collaborate to solve problems and achieve goals. A growth mindset also encourages open communication and the willingness to learn from others. "

" Additionally, the growth mindset does not only exists in the I.T. field, it also exists in other areas, for example, in other businesses or fields that exist in this era. In this modern generation and today's world, a growth mindset can be a powerful tool for success and personal growth. This school of thought believes that rather than being fixed characteristics, intelligence, and ability can be developed through hard work and dedication. It motivates people to try new things, persevere in the face of adversity, and look for new learning opportunities. "

" Increased resilience is one of the advantages of a growth mindset. Resilience is essential in today's rapidly changing world for adapting to new situations and recovering from setbacks. Failures and setbacks are viewed as opportunities to learn and grow by those with a growth mindset, rather than as indicators of their limitations. They are also more likely to persevere and remain motivated in the face of adversity. Another advantage of having a growth mindset is improved learning. Instead of avoiding challenges, this mindset encourages people to seek out new learning opportunities and embrace them. Those with a growth mindset can learn new skills and knowledge by valuing effort and dedication, even in areas where they previously struggled. "

" A growth mindset can also promote creativity and innovation. Because they value new ideas and perspectives, those with a growth mindset can approach problems from various perspectives and develop novel solutions. This is particularly useful in fields where innovation is essential, such as technology, entrepreneurship, or the arts. Finally, a growth mindset can assist individuals in achieving greater success and personal growth outside of their professional careers. Individuals who embrace challenges, persevere in the face of adversity, and value learning and collaboration are more likely to achieve their objectives and realize their full potential. Individuals who have a growth mindset can help others develop the resilience, creativity, and collaborative skills needed for success in today's world, whether in personal or professional endeavors "
